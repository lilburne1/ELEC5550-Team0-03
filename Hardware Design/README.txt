This folder includes the hardware design for ELEC5550 Team0-03's FSO communication system.
This includes:
    The KiCad schematic and PCB manufacturing files for the laser receiver circuit
    The KiCad schematic and PCB manufacturing files for the laser driver circuit
    The KiCad schematic and PCB manufacturing files for the mainboards
    The 3D printing and CAD files for the system's enclosure and laser lens holder
